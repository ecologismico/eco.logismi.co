AgMIP

Agricultural Model Intercomparison and Improvement Project

International grant of UKAid through USDA, ICRISAT and Univ of Columbia
2012-2014

AgMIP is a distributed climate-scenario simulation exercise for historical model intercomparison and future climate change conditions with participation of multiple crop and agricultural economics modeling groups around the world. The goals of AgMIP are to improve substantially the characterization of risk of hunger and world food security due to climate change and to enhance adaptation capacity in both developing and developed countries.

We are mainly involved with the IT team and the East Africa project.
